Key features of Xynage

Wide Range of Media Support

Xynage helps you to gather attention of more customers. It allows you to add wide range of media and content such as images, videos, twitter posts, and more all on a single screen.

Effortless Menu Board Creation

Create interactive and personalized content with simple drag and drop tool of the CMS. Also, you can offer a great customer experience by overlaying video over image or vice-versa.

Central Management

Decentralized content can be managed either via your own CMS or using our hassle-free Cloud Hosting. You can remotely control and manage the content with zero maintenance.

Automatic Scheduling

Flexible scheduling allows changes in content on daily, weekly and monthly basis automatically. Event based schedules are perfect for running new product, special event or festival campaigns.

Offline Storage

Xynage can display scheduled content without Internet. Content and schedule updates are pushed from local storages to the players automatically when Internet connection resumes.

Simple Plug-and-Play Installation

Xynage is a simple plug-and-play digital signage solution where all you need is a display device, an HDMI cable, and an internet connection.
